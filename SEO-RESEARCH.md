# Pitch Perfect SEO Research & Keyword Strategy

## Competitor Analysis

| Competitor | Type | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|---|---|---|---|
| **musictheory.net** | Web | Free, structured, printable reports, trusted brand | No gamification, minimal interactivity |
| **Teoría** | Web | Deep harmonic analysis, rigorous exercises | Dated design, academic feel |
| **TonedEar** | Web | Gamified, engaging, customizable | Basic theory lessons |
| **Perfect Ear** | Mobile | Feature-rich, all-in-one, teacher-recommended | Mobile only, freemium |
| **ToneGym** | Web | Gamified, leaderboards, community | Pro subscription needed for full access |
| **EarMaster** | Cross-platform | Used by music schools, structured | Expensive full version |
| **Functional Ear Trainer** | Mobile | Unique scale-degree approach | Limited exercise types |

**Pitch Perfect's differentiator**: 100% free web-based ear training with gamification, Tone.js-powered audio, no account required, works on all devices.
